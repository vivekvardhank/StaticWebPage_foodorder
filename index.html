Mini Pizzas

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a savory chicken and rice dish that includes layers of chicken, rice, and aromatics that are steamed together.

Panna Cotta

Panna cotta (Italian for "cooked cream") is an Italian dessert of sweetened cream thickened with gelatin and molded.

Qubani ka Meetha

Qubani ka Meetha also known as Khubani ka meetha is a popular sweet dish from Hyderabadi cuisine made from dried apricots, sugar and nuts.

Mutton Dalcha

Dalcha with mutton and kaddu is yet another Hyderabadi cuisine often made with meat or mutton mixed in bottle gourd (kaddu) and dal(lentils)
